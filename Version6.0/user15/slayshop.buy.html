Tangy is a peppy cat villager that appears in every game of the Animal Crossing series. The name "Tangy" comes from the sharp taste of citrus fruit. Her Japanese name is a contraction of "100 Percent" and refers to juice concentrate. She appears on the album cover for K.K. Birthday. She has the music hobby.

Mint

Mint is a snooty squirrel villager from the Animal Crossing series, known for her mint green fur. She appears in nearly every game and references mint in her quotes. Mint has a fashion hobby and is featured on the cover of Animal City.

Raymond

Raymond is a smug cat villager from New Horizons and Pocket Camp. His English name means "Advice," reflecting his business attire. His Japanese phrase suggests strictness, while his Dutch phrase means neat, highlighting his precision. He has a nature hobby.

Lolly

Lolly is a normal cat villager in the Animal Crossing series, introduced in Animal Forest e+. Her name, derived from "lollipop," and her catchphrase "bonbon," which means "candy" in French, reflect her sweet personality. Her Japanese name, Ramune, refers to a type of soda, and both her name and phrases often refer to cats.
