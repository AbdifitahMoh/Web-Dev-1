Bugatti Veyron

The Veyron represents the pinnacle of automotive engineering. With its quad-turbo W16 engine, it set the world record for the fastest car, reaching 267 mph.

  • Top Speed: 267 mph
  • Horsepower: 1,001 hp
  • 0-60 mph: 2.5 seconds
  • Engine: 8.0L Quad-Turbocharged W16

Features: Full leather interior, advanced aerodynamics, carbon fiber body, and a computer-controlled active suspension system. The Veyron redefined performance with unmatched precision engineering.

The Divo is a more track-focused version of the Chiron, built for precision and agility, making it an ideal choice for those who seek even more performance on the track.
